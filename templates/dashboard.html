Customer Engagement Summary

Based on the dashboard, customer engagement showed a slight overall decline over time. This is reflected in the "Active Households by Week" chart, where the number of active households gradually decreased from 2018 to 2020. Although the "Average Spend per Household" slightly increased during some periods (especially around mid-2020), the drop in total active households suggests that fewer customers were consistently shopping. This decline in participation, even with stable or slightly higher individual spending, indicates an overall reduction in engagement.
